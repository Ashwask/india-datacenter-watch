# Overview

India Datacenter Watch is a transparency project: a map plus an open dataset of data-center
facilities across India, each tied to a public source.

## Why

Data centers are critical infrastructure, but they consume power, water, and land that local
communities also depend on. In India this is acute:

- **Energy:** ~0.5% of national electricity demand today, expected to reach 1–2% as AI compute scales.
- **Water:** cooling draw projected to rise from ~150 billion litres (2025) to ~358 billion by 2030;
  WRI India finds >50% of India's data centers are in water-stressed regions.
- **Land & siting:** hyperscale campuses are rising in groundwater-stressed districts such as
  Visakhapatnam, often with little public disclosure of resource use.

## Status taxonomy

| status              | meaning                                              |
|---------------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| `operational`       | Live and running per the operator / reporting        |
| `under_construction`| Announced and building                               |
| `proposed`          | Announced / MoU / planned, not yet building          |
| `community_reported`| Submitted by a resident, pending verification        |
| `decommissioned`    | Retired                                              |
