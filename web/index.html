About

India Datacenter Watch is an open, community-accountability project mapping every data center we can find across India — and the energy, water, land, and community costs of the buildout.

Why this exists

Capacity is growing faster than public scrutiny of its resource use. Operators rarely disclose facility-level water and power figures, leaving communities to estimate the impact. This project puts the facilities, the numbers, and the reporting in one public place.

How it's built

A sourced open dataset — one row per facility, every row cited — drives the map, live statistics, and transparent impact estimates. Coordinates fall back to city/cluster centroids where exact siting isn't public, and that's noted on each marker.

How you can help

Spot a facility we're missing, or seeing local impact? File a community report with photos. We review every submission before it appears on the map as community-reported, and promote verified facilities into the dataset.

FAQ

What is India Datacenter Watch?

An open, community-accountability map of data centers across India — operational, under construction, proposed, and resident-reported — with a public source link for every facility.

Where does the data come from?

From operator websites and public reporting (e.g. datacentermap, DatacenterDynamics, company press releases, and news). Every facility row carries a source URL you can open from its map popup.

How accurate are the locations?

Where exact siting is published we use it. For cloud "regions" and campuses whose precise address is not public, markers are placed at the city/cluster centroid — and the popup notes say so.

What does "water-stressed" mean here?

It flags facilities in regions identified as water-stressed (e.g. by WRI India). It is a watch flag for where cooling demand meets scarce water — not a measured per-facility figure.

How do I add or correct a facility?

Use the report form below. Submissions are reviewed before appearing on the map as community-reported.

About the data. This is an open, best-effort dataset compiled from operator websites and public reporting. Facility coordinates are placed at city/cluster level where exact siting is not public, and IT-load figures are shown only where reported. Smaller, captive, edge, and unannounced facilities may still be missing. Corrections welcome — file a report above.
