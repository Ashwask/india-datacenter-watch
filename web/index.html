Key concerns

Data centers are critical digital infrastructure — and they draw real power, water, and land from the communities around them. India's operational stock crossed ~1,520 MW IT in 2025 (+34% YoY) and is projected to triple by 2030. These are the impacts worth watching.

Energy Consumption

Data centers are ~0.5% of India's electricity demand today, expected to reach 1–2% as the AI buildout accelerates — concentrated on a handful of urban grids.

Water Usage

Cooling draw is projected to rise from ~150 billion litres (2025) to ~358 billion by 2030. A WRI India analysis found more than half of India's data centers sit in water-stressed regions.

FAQ

What is India Datacenter Watch?

An open, community-accountability map of data centers across India — operational, under construction, proposed, and resident-reported — with a public source link for every facility. It is inspired by the Brockovich Data Center map for the US.

Where does the data come from?

From operator websites and public reporting (e.g. datacentermap, DatacenterDynamics, company press releases, and news). Every facility row carries a source URL you can open from its map popup.

How accurate are the locations?

Where exact siting is published we use it. For cloud "regions" and campuses whose precise address is not public, markers are placed at the city/cluster centroid — and the popup notes say so.

What does "water-stressed" mean here?

It flags facilities in regions identified as water-stressed (e.g. by WRI India). It is a watch flag for where cooling demand meets scarce water — not a measured per-facility figure.

How do I add or correct a facility?

Use the report form below. Submissions are reviewed before appearing on the map as community-reported.

Report an Issue

Add a facility we're missing, or document local impact you're witnessing. This static site has no server, so your report is packaged locally — email it to us or download it to attach to a GitHub issue. We review every submission before it goes on the map as community-reported.

📷 Photo uploads aren't supported on this static demo — please mention photos in your report and attach them to the email or GitHub issue (max 5, JPG/PNG, 10 MB each).

About the data. This is an open, best-effort dataset compiled from operator websites and public reporting. Facility coordinates are placed at city/cluster level where exact siting is not public, and IT-load figures are shown only where reported. Smaller, captive, edge, and unannounced facilities may still be missing. Corrections welcome — file a report above.
