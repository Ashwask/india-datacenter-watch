Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026

No tracking

This site sets no tracking cookies and runs no analytics or advertising scripts. Map tiles are served by OpenStreetMap/CARTO and Leaflet/Chart.js assets by public CDNs; loading them exposes your IP address to those providers, as with any website.

The report form

If this deployment has no backend configured, the form transmits nothing automatically: "Email instead" opens your own mail client with details pre-filled, and "Download JSON" saves the report to your device.

If a backend (Cloudflare Worker) is configured, clicking "Submit report" sends the fields and any photos you attach to that backend, where they are stored unapproved until a moderator reviews them. Only approved submissions are shown publicly. We use any contact details you provide solely to follow up on your report. You can ask us to delete your submission.

Photos

Photos you upload are stored to serve the public gallery only after approval. Don't upload images containing other people's private information or anything you don't have the right to share.

Contact

For corrections, takedowns, or data requests, open an issue on GitHub.
