Facility locations, statuses, and capacities may be approximate, incomplete, or out of date.

Nothing here is legal, investment, environmental, or professional advice. Do not rely on it for siting, compliance, commercial, or regulatory decisions without independent verification.

Community submissions

By submitting a community report you confirm the information is true to the best of your knowledge, that you have the right to share any photos you upload, and that your submission (text and photos) may be reviewed and, if verified, published publicly on the map. Do not submit confidential information or content that infringes others' rights. We may decline, edit, or remove any submission.

Impact estimates

Energy, water, CO₂, and e-waste figures are transparent estimates derived from disclosed IT load using stated assumptions (PUE, WUE, grid factor). They are illustrative, not measured per-facility values.
